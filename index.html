About Me

Hi there! My name is a William Kong and I'm currently a mathematics student specializing in mathematical finance at the University of Waterloo. On occasion I will be working in Toronto, Canada, for some of my co-operative terms.

My current interests in mathematics include computational mathematics, optimization theory, stochastic calculus, probability theory, and statistics. Other than mathematics, I have interests in algorithms, functional programming, anime and manga, guitar, and music theory.
